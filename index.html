About Us

La Cantina

Welcome to La Cantina. Since 2016, offering traditional dishes of the highest quality.

We buy from a local vendor that are produce experts. We choose fresh and seasonal products first; when available we purchase organic. Alongside the normal menu, there are vegetarian meals. Our chefs try their best to accommodate special diets, it is best to alert them beforehand so they can assist with any dietary needs or restrictions.

Frequently Asked Questions

FAQ

What is the payment method? Is cash allowed?

One of two ways to eat at cantina is entering your pin number and then your intra username. For example, if your pin was 4242 and your intra username was "student" you would enter "4242student" on the keyboard and press enter. The second way is entering your pin number and than scanning your student ID card.

What is the payment method? Is cash allowed?

You can load up your cantina balance online by logging in and using your credit or debit card. You cannot use cash as everything must be preloaded onto your balance before you purchase any meals from the cantina.

How can I restore my Cantina pin number?

You can change and fix your cantina pin number by going to sesame and changing your pin to any four digit numeric value of your choice. If you forget the steps you can private message marvin on slack with the factoid command !sesame for the steps to do so.

Can I leave my dishes and trash on the table when I‚Äôm done?

No, you must bus your own table using the disposable bins at the entrance/exit of the cantina. Dispose all compostable items (food waste, paper) in the green bin and all other trash in the dark bin.

What are the Marvin commands for ?

Here are the commands that you can use in slack. Please use these in a private message with @marvin!

  • To check the open hours, use: !cantina
  • To check the menu, use: !menu
  • For help with pin, use !sesame

Do you offer veggie, gluten-free, or other special diets?

Alongside the normal menu there is a vegetarian meal that is available. Our chefs try their best to accommodate special diets, it is best to alert them beforehand so they can assist with any dietary needs or restrictions.
